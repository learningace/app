Unified Solution for online education
Overview

LearningAce, is a unified platfrom for delivering online course and distance education. It facilitates all the needs of the online learning community and benefits the teachers as well as students by bringing them on a common platform, thereby bringing unified learning at your convenience. LearningAce is driven by a concept of Dashboards for all the stakeholders in order to facilitate the e-Learning process. A one-stop view to all the important parameters a user needs to have an insight of are available as soon as they log into the application.

Highlights
  • Dashboards for all users
  • Anywhere, anytime access to content
  • Self-paced, Interactive learning
  • Customized reports and progress indicators
  • Responsive design for ease of viewing across devices
  • Create question banks & assessments
